Rugby is New Zealand’s National Sport and we’d even go so far as saying that it’s part of our identity. It’s a huge part of life here! Not only is it played in schools and clubs around the country, but many children’s wildest dreams are of playing for the All Blacks - the New Zealand team. The All Blacks won the Rugby World Cup in 1987, 2011 and 2015 and as of 2020 are currently rated as the 3rd best team in the world.

Cricket is played from a professional level right down to backyard cricket in every garden or at every beach BBQ throughout the country in the summer. You better get ready to have a game while you’re here! The national team are nicknamed the Blackcaps (a nod to the All Blacks) and are currently (as of February 2021) ranked as the best test cricket team in the world.

Tennis in New Zealand is an ideal sport for those who want to play at a professional level. There are different kinds of tournaments that are organized in New Zealand, and they are aimed at various age groups. New Zealand is known for its great players like Michael Venus, Andy Roddick, Lleyton Hewitt, and Wayne Ferreira. The best time to play tennis in New Zealand is during the winter season.

New Zealand is home to one of the world’s most exciting sports, and the country has a long history of success in the sport. Basketball is played at all levels in New Zealand, from club to international level. New Zealand is the best place to play basketball in the world. There are numerous basketball courts in New Zealand, which are made of cement and asphalt. You can play basketball at any time of the day.

New Zealand is one of the smallest countries in the world and it has only a population of 4.5 million. However, it has produced some of the best baseball players in the world. New Zealand’s national baseball team is ranked fourth in the world and has won many international tournaments. The players are all highly skilled and are considered to be one of the best teams in the world.

Football is one of the most popular sports in New Zealand. In New Zealand, the football league has eight teams, each representing a region. The most successful team is the Auckland City Football Club. It is one of the countries that are home to some of the best footballers in the world. New Zealand national football team is also one of the best teams in the world.
